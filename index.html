Our Services

Online Ordering

Order online and get your food delivered faster than light.
Get food delivered right where you are in the campus. We take online orders for students between 12:00 PM to 2 PM on all working days. The main idea of ordering food online through us is to limit the excess production of food leading them into garbage. Also, in monsoon it is not accessible for everyone to go and take food, hence we are there for you.
We accept payments through UPI, Debit/Credit Card only. COD will be available soon.

Take Aways

Why to stand in line when you can just take away?
Order your food through our website and just take away from the canteen itself. This would help us to make the desired quantity of food on request and hence would reduce any wastage. This service is available any time on all working days.
We accept payments in this mode through cash and UPI only. .
